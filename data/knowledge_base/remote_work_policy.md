# Remote Work Policy

## Eligibility
Employees who have completed their 3-month probationary period are eligible to work remotely up to 3 days per week. Remote work eligibility is subject to role requirements and manager approval.

## Equipment Allowance
The company provides a one-time equipment allowance of $500 for eligible remote workers to set up a suitable home office. Receipts must be submitted within 60 days of the allowance being granted.

## Core Hours
All remote employees must be available and responsive during core hours: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM in their local time zone. Meetings scheduled during core hours take priority over other activities.

## VPN Requirements
Remote employees must connect to the company VPN whenever accessing internal systems, databases, or confidential documents. VPN credentials are provisioned by IT on the first day of employment.

## Communication Expectations
Remote employees are expected to join video calls with camera on unless otherwise agreed. Slack status must reflect availability accurately. Response to messages is expected within 2 hours during working hours.

## Security
Remote work must be conducted on a password-protected network. Public Wi-Fi may only be used with the company VPN active. Laptop screens must not be visible to others when handling sensitive information.
