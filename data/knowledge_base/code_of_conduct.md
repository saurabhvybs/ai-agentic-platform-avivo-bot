# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to maintaining a respectful, inclusive, and professional workplace. All employees, contractors, and visitors are expected to treat one another with dignity regardless of background, identity, or role.

## Expected Behavior
- Communicate respectfully and constructively in all professional interactions.
- Collaborate openly and share knowledge generously with colleagues.
- Respect confidentiality of company information and colleague privacy.
- Arrive on time for meetings and honor commitments.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation of any kind — verbal, written, or physical.
- Discrimination based on race, gender, religion, age, disability, or sexual orientation.
- Sharing confidential company data with unauthorized parties.
- Use of company resources for personal gain or inappropriate purposes.

## Reporting Violations
Employees who witness or experience violations of this Code of Conduct should report them to their HR Business Partner or through the anonymous Ethics Hotline at ethics@company.com. All reports are treated confidentially. Retaliation against reporters is strictly prohibited.

## Consequences
Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment, depending on severity and context.
