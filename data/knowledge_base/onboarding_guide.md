# Onboarding Guide

## First Day
Your first day begins with an orientation session at 9:00 AM with your HR Business Partner. You will receive your laptop, company ID badge, and access credentials. IT will spend 30 minutes setting up your machine and configuring VPN, Slack, and email.

## First Week Schedule
- Day 1: HR orientation, IT setup, office tour, meet your team
- Day 2: Role-specific training with your line manager, review of 90-day goals
- Day 3: Shadow a senior team member, join team standup and sprint ceremonies
- Day 4: Begin starter tasks assigned in Jira, attend company all-hands (if scheduled)
- Day 5: 1:1 check-in with manager, complete mandatory compliance training

## Tools and Access
- **Slack**: Primary communication tool. Join #general, #your-team, and #announcements channels.
- **Jira**: Task and project tracking. Your manager will add you to the relevant boards.
- **GitHub**: Source code. Request access via your team lead.
- **Confluence**: Internal documentation wiki. Use the search bar to find policies and guides.
- **HR Portal**: Submit leave requests, expense claims, and access payslips.

## Who to Contact
- IT helpdesk: it-support@company.com or Slack #it-help
- HR queries: hr@company.com or your assigned HR Business Partner
- Payroll questions: payroll@company.com
- Office access/badge: facilities@company.com

## 90-Day Goals
Your manager will set 3–5 goals for your first 90 days covering: technical onboarding, relationship building, and an initial contribution to a team project. These are reviewed at 30, 60, and 90-day check-ins.
