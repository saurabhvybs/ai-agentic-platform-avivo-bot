# Leave Policy

## Annual Leave
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 working days of paid annual leave per year. Part-time employees receive leave on a pro-rata basis. Leave accrues monthly at 1.67 days per month from the employee's start date.

## Sick Leave
Employees may take up to 10 days of paid sick leave per calendar year. A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days. Unused sick leave does not carry over to the following year.

## Maternity Leave
Eligible employees are entitled to 16 weeks of paid maternity leave, beginning up to 4 weeks before the expected due date. To qualify, the employee must have been employed for at least 12 months.

## Paternity Leave
New fathers and co-parents are entitled to 2 weeks of paid paternity leave, to be taken within 8 weeks of the child's birth or adoption.

## Carry-Over Rules
Up to 5 days of unused annual leave may be carried over to the following year. Carried-over leave must be used by March 31 of the new year or it will be forfeited. No carry-over is permitted for sick leave.

## Leave Request Process
All leave requests must be submitted via the HR portal at least 5 working days in advance for planned leave. Emergency leave must be reported to the line manager on the first day of absence. Approval is subject to operational requirements.
