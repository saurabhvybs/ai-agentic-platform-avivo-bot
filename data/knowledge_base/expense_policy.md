# Expense Policy

## Reimbursable Expenses
The company reimburses reasonable business expenses including: travel (economy class for flights under 6 hours), accommodation (up to $200/night), meals during business travel (up to $75/day), and client entertainment with prior approval.

## Approval Workflow
Expenses up to $100 require line manager approval only. Expenses between $100 and $500 require line manager plus Finance team approval. Expenses above $500 require VP-level sign-off before being incurred.

## Submission Deadline
Expense claims must be submitted within 30 days of the expense being incurred. Claims submitted after 60 days will not be reimbursed except in exceptional circumstances approved by Finance.

## Required Documentation
All expense claims must include: original receipts (PDF or clear photo), business purpose, names of attendees for meals/entertainment, and the project or cost center code.

## Reimbursement Timeline
Approved expenses are reimbursed within 10 business days via direct bank transfer to the employee's registered account.

## Non-Reimbursable Items
The following are not reimbursable: personal expenses, alcohol (unless part of an approved client entertainment event), upgrades beyond economy class without pre-approval, and fines or penalties.
